Gensler’s Design Forecast events are a series of hyper-local conversations with our clients and the communities where we practice. Each of these 45+ local gatherings explore the forces impacting the human experience in that city. The series culminates with Design Forecast LIVE, a bi-annual global summit meant to share ideas and co-create the future.

Gensler impacts millions of people's lives every day by focusing on the human experience of cities, and by taking on the toughest challenges facing the world’s urban centers, including climate change, mobility, the connected city, and issues of housing and homelessness. Gensler’s Design Forecast presents a comprehensive look at the design solutions and strategies we use to take on these tough challenges. Fueled by insights, this annual publication provides over 100 pages of data, research, and industry trends.
