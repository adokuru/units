Impact by Design 2018 is Gensler’s third annual publication assessing the performance and resilience of our work. As the world’s leading architecture and design firm, we have an unrivaled opportunity to help our clients and communities around the world improve the impact of the built environment.

In this year’s report, we introduce six strategies for positive impact that we believe have the greatest potential to improve the resilience of the environment in the coming years. We hope this report serves as a reminder of the unique ability of the design industry to create real value in people’s lives by adapting to a changing environment.

Download the full report or explore Gensler’s Strategies for Positive Impact in the articles below.

SHAPING THE FUTURE OF CITIES
The built environment can help achieve a new era of global wellness. Addressing the climate crisis is more important than ever. Resilient design enriches the human experience, promotes well-being, and ensures safety.

As the world’s largest design firm, we have a unique obligation and opportunity to improve the impact of the built environment — but also to lead the industry. We challenge all our colleagues and clients to join us in the path toward carbon neutrality.
