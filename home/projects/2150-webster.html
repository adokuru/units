2150 Webster

Oakland, CA

2150 Webster Street is a ten-story commercial office building with 230,000 square feet of rentable floor area. Originally constructed in 1976, the building was restored to a refined and more honest expression of materiality, with the exterior clad in a new metal panel rain screen and all-new glazing systems integrated with the metal panels to increase the building's energy efficiency and access to natural light. On the interior, the lobby was refreshed with a contemporary entry lobby and a lounge area that provides access and connectivity to the ground floor café and retail space. The addition of a new rooftop terrace with a bar offers intimate, panoramic views of Oakland.
