Aperture Del Mar

San Diego, California

Aperture Del Mar is a world class campus that is designed to embody the southern California lifestyle by blending smart, hospitality inspired spaces, and distinguished architectural design. Aperture offers companies flexibility and scalability of office space ranging from 100,000 to 630,000 square feet. The campus is truly unique in that each building and floor has connectivity to the outdoor environment. Multiple balconies, pocket doors, and exterior stairs and bridges connect the campus to create the ultimate user experience and collision points at every level. The outdoor spaces blend seamlessly with the indoor spaces that feature dining, event lawn, walking paths, an amphitheater, indoor and outdoor fitness, as well as access to over 4,000 acres of running, biking and hiking trails.
