Adobe Dublin

Dublin, Ireland

Having worked on a number of projects for Adobe around the world, Gensler was challenged to create a space for its Dublin office that would encapsulate and amplify its brand – as we had successfully done with its London flagship. The new workspace needed to facilitate collaboration and support new ways of working, whilst being future-proofed to adapt to its growing business needs. Split into two main open-plan working zones, the space features a variety of meeting and focus rooms, as well as a kitchen in the heart of the space with an adjoining lounge comprised of modular sofas. Wellbeing was at the forefront for the design team, who made sure that there was enough time and space for the life-work balance to ebb and flow naturally, accommodating growth and social movement within the office.
