American Multinational Financial Services Corporation

Boston, Massachusetts

In response to a changing market, this financial services organization developed a strategy to ensure consistent experiences of its spaces, services and messaging, and came to Gensler for a comprehensive redesign that aimed at driving employee engagement and enhancing client loyalty. First implemented in its bustling Boston headquarters, the new design creates a sophisticated, welcoming first impression. Guest zones are replete with functional, flexible work areas. A centralized amenity hub supports employee productivity and work/life balance with an IT help-desk bar, touchdown workplace, full-service concierge desk, and health and wellness center.
