The Alexander

Indianapolis, Indiana

The Alexander raises the bar when it comes to an authentic, guest-focused lodging experience. With 209 guest and extended stay rooms, the building also features a restaurant and bar, a lobby large enough to house events, fitness and conference centers and outdoor meeting areas. Seeking LEED® certification, the hotel incorporates an energy- and water-conservation system, including storm water collection and irrigation and a 2,500-gallon cistern. Enlivening the hotel’s public spaces are generous contemporary art collections, curated by the Indianapolis Museum of Art, adding fluency to the design’s blend of form and function.
