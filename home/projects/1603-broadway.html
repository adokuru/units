1603 Broadway

San Antonio, Texas

As one of the first vertical mixed-use developments of this scale in San Antonio, 1603 Broadway is a transformative project, acting as a catalyst by bringing new life to the neighborhood and connecting the Broadway corridor to downtown. The building, one of several planned developments which are part of a larger Broadway revitalization, is a foundational piece in establishing the new character of the district. The Class-A office space and international luxury hotel flag at 1603 Broadway draws upon the vibrancy of the adjacent Pearl District, while also acting as an extension of the downtown core. Rich in amenities, the building offers office tenants access to ground-floor retail and restaurants, as well as hotel facilities such as a spa and fitness center. The floorplates provide deeper-than-usual office bays while soaring ceilings allow for increased flexibility and open layouts coveted by tech and creative tenants.
