AON

London, UK

The Aon Centre is a next-generation headquarters — a radical step forward that positions the global insurance firm as a progressive market leader. Embedded in the epicentre of London’s insurance world, the project facilitates an exceptional visitor experience and provides a dynamic workplace environment that fosters highly mobile and collaborative ways of working. Gensler’s clean-lined design embraces the base build architecture, whilst embodying Aon’s values and culture to maximise brand visibility and position the firm as a progressive market leader.
