400 Channelside

Tampa, Florida

A premiere mixed-use building in the Water Street Tampa redevelopment, 400 Channelside is conceived as a gateway and catalyst for urban engagement, bound by Channelside Drive on the south, South Morgan Street on the west, South Jefferson Street on the east, and East Brorein Street on the north. The tower creates an everyday experience that heightens mental and physical wellness and offers easy access to the outdoors with a landscaped sky garden that provides areas suitable for meetings, individual work, social events, or as respite to rejuvenate.
