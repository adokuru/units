Akerman Brickell City Centre

Miami, Florida

A strong presence in Miami is imperative to Akerman’s diverse practice across Latin America. The law firm needed a new space to support growth, and Gensler’s knowledge of the practice and the broader legal industry helped them evaluate the best real estate options. The new space in the Brickell City Centre Green building provides Akerman an efficient new workplace that decreases filing and storage area, integrates more open and collaborative space, and features striking new finishes, all while decreasing the office's overall square footage. The new office design increases productivity with flex zones on each floor, cafe space with communal tables, and a conference center that incorporates cutting-edge technology.
