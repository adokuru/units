Amgen

Thousand Oaks, California

Amgen’s Workplace of the Future is the result of a new way of looking at how the workspace can support optimal workstyles and efficiency, as well as improve performance globally. With innovation and collaboration between teams key components of the biopharmaceutical company’s success, Gensler developed a project strategy based on the concept of community, addressing Amgen’s functional and organizational goals and providing a direction for new standards and global implementation. The re-invention of an existing 40,000-square-foot building as a LEED® Gold-certified project served as the pilot for Amgen’s global initiative.
