Andaz Savannah

Savannah, Georgia

Andaz Savannah is an eclectic boutique hotel in the heart of Savannah Georgia with artful décor, relaxed urban vibe, and new South sophistication. The hotel’s vibrant, modern feel provides visitors with a place to relax, work, dine, and connect just steps from the Historic District. Inspired by Savannah’s eclectic patchwork of city squares, the rooms are designed to feel like a posh Savannah apartment, with unique details, the comfort, and ease of a home, and an authentic sense of place. Drawing from the way Savannah’s colorful buildings seem to burst from the haze after a midday rain shower, the design incorporated a color palette of blush and jewel tones set against crisp white or gray backgrounds.
