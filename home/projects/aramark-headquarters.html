Aramark Headquarters

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

When Aramark relocated its headquarters to 2400 Market Street, the company worked with Gensler on a design that emboldens the company’s Philadelphia presence, personifies its brand and vision, and showcases its innovative offerings to their associates, clients, and the community. The design team introduced an agile workplace strategy with diverse spaces to fit different types of work throughout the day. A balance of assigned and unassigned seating options, such as lounge and collaboration areas, focus rooms, and team rooms, offer variety and choice and enhance productivity. The amenities floor boasts a state-of-the-art exhibition and development kitchen and a culinary food hall. A world-class fitness center and outdoor terraces offer a respite from the workday, encouraging employees to nourish their body, mind, and spirit.
