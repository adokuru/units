25 Kent

Brooklyn, New York

25 Kent is the first ground-up commercial office development in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, in more than 40 years. The building captures the neighborhood’s character and industrial heritage, while addressing contemporary trends with workspace layouts and design suitable for growing, cutting-edge technology, and light industrial and manufacturing companies. As Design Development Architect, Gensler expanded upon a design by Hollwich Kushner, Design Architect. Street-level frontage will have local retail outposts serving the building occupants, as well as the community. 25 Kent will house a community of innovative and creative businesses integrated into this vibrant neighborhood.
