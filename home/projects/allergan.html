Allergan

Madison, New Jersey

Allergan consolidated four of its New Jersey operations into a single campus at 5 Giralda Farms in Madison, New Jersey as Allergan's new U.S. Administrative Headquarters. The design reinforces Allergan’s BOLD culture. It has collaborative social spaces and amenities designed to build stronger bridges between colleagues as the company develops and delivers innovative products for patients around the world. The transformative workplace is mostly open with a variety of meeting spaces. There are new amenities for employees, including a conference center, an employee cafe, coffee bar, a company store with Allergan products, a wellness center, fitness center, outdoor courtyards, covered parking, and access to bike and walking trails.
