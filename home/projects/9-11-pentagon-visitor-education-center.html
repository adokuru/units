9/11 Pentagon Visitor Education Center

Arlington County, Virginia

In recognition of the fact that the Pentagon is the only 9/11 attack site without full commemoration, the Pentagon Memorial Fund is working to create a 9/11 Pentagon Visitor Education Center. As part of the fundraising effort, Gensler created a series of renderings and a capital campaign book that illustrate the potential visitor experience to prospective donors. The package provides context and helps position the Center as a vehicle telling the story of the 9/11 attack in a timeless, respectful manner.
