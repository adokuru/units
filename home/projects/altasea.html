AltaSea

San Pedro, California

This public-private partnership is transforming 35 acres of land at the Port of Los Angeles into an ocean-side innovation campus that will bring education, science, business and the community together and inspire future generations to pursue a balanced, sustainable relationship between civilization and nature. The project won a 2016 Westside Urban Forum Award Citation in the unbuilt category.
